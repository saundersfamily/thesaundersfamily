About Us

We are a family of 5 plus one dog. Sheila and I are the parents who met at a payphone. Ask one of us the full story about our first meeting and be prepared to laugh...mostly at me (Anthony). Our life is spent at ball fields and tracks across the country. You can see a map of all the states that we have visited for one sport or another on the extracurriculars page. We do other stuff but none of it is as fun as watching our kids compete in their respective sports.

Anthony

I am the old man and captain of this ship. I am techie at heart and love to learn new stuff on my computer. As an example, this website was born when I decided I wanted to learn about the CSS Grid (website nerd language) when we were ordered to stay home during the Covid-19 pandemic. I also like to read books by Vince Flynn, Lee Child and Brad Thor. Finally, I do skills videos for athletes to send to coaches as part of the recruiting process. And before someone gets all bent out of shape, Sheila actually calls the shots most of the time since she manages the cash.

Sheila

If Anthony is the old man and captain of the ship, then I guess I must be the semi-old, co-captain. I love traveling with the beach always being my first choice of destinations. I keep saying that once the kids are gone, we might actually have money to get a little bungalow on the beach somewhere. I love watching our kids grow and develop not just in their respective sports but more importantly in the classroom and life skills. My "tribe" of best friends keeps me grounded and provides me with opportunities for stress relief and laughter. I absolutely love our crazy life and wouldn't have it any other way.

I recently transferred to Ohio State where I plan to major in Psychology with a minor in sociology and education. I take my academics very seriously and in my opinion, I am the smartest of the three kids. I am excited to move out on my own this summer. If you want to give me a house warming gift...I won't say no.
